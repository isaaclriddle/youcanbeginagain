## Ain’t That a Kick in the Head: A Theoretical Reorientation through *Fallout: New Vegas*

This paper is an exploration of one of the games housed within this archive, *Fallout: New Vegas*. It serves an inquiry into the gameplay and narrative systems that allow the game’s player character, Courier Six, to serve as a trans embodied object through which players can explore their gender identities. In deciding to analyze *Fallout: New Vegas* rather than the archive’s objects, I aim to highlight the capacity for trans players to use virtual avatars to navigate their gender identities without projecting my own experiences as a trans person onto the archive’s participants. This analysis, then, aims to provide a potential theoretical framework for understanding the stories contained within *You Can Begin Again.*
